# Rock, Paper, Scissors Battle

Two players choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors. A round begins once both players have locked in a move. The hands shake, then the chosen moves are revealed.

Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock. If both players choose the same move, the round is a tie and no points are added.

The winner of each round gets one point. The Reset button clears the score and starts the game over.

Player 1 can use the on-screen buttons or the A, S, and D keys for Rock, Paper, and Scissors. Player 2 can use the on-screen buttons or the 1, 2, and 3 keys.
